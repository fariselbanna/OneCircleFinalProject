About Dominic Dabish

Dominic Dabish is an experienced software engineer in the Greater Detroit area. He earned graduate and undergraduate degrees in Computer Science from Oakland University. With experience at organizations like the U.S. Department of Defense and PwC, Dominic continues to excel as a professional, educator, and entrepreneur.

Beyond his technical expertise, Dominic is an Adjunct Professor of Computer Science at Lawrence Technological University. He has a passion for mentoring students and advancing technology-enhanced learning, and he founded the "Coding with Dom" platform to empower learners worldwide.

He is also the Managing Partner of Dabish Ventures, focusing on innovative startups, and President of the World Computing Organization, helping shape the future of technology professionals globally.
